Tea atelier

I was asked to redesign the website of a small tea atelier based in Bogot√°, Colombia. This small business is a place where tea and its culture are the main focus along with other activities that promote a quiet life. The owners believe it is possible to take daily life one cup of tea at a time.


Competitor analysis

I conducted a SWOT (Strenghts, Weaknesses, Opportunities and Threats) analysis between some of the most representative business in the area. I evaluated how they sell their products and services online and possible features to have in the Tea Atelier website.


User research

To know more about tea drinkers in the area, I conducted some user research. Some of the respondents were very knowledgeable about tea, while others had just been introduced to this culture. However, gathered insights from different profiles of tea enthusiasts helped me discover some of their concerns about buying tea and what they wanted to know about the tea culture.


App Map

To further understand how the existent apps are addressing the issue of motivating users I conducted a competitor analysis amongst some popular fitness products. This let me discover useful features and pros and cons in the competition.
